Information We Collect

LeafLens is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our plant identification app.

Photos and Images

  • Plant Photos: When you use plant identification features, we process your photos through our AI system to identify plants. Photos are not permanently stored unless you choose to save them to your collection.
  • Gallery Access: We access your photo library to allow you to select existing photos for plant identification.
  • Camera Access: We use your device's camera to capture photos for plant identification.

Device Information

  • Basic Device Data: We collect basic device information to ensure optimal app performance and troubleshoot issues.
  • App Usage: We collect anonymous usage data to improve our services and user experience.
  • Technical Data: Information about app crashes, performance, and system compatibility.

How We Use Your Information

Plant Identification Services

  • AI Processing: Your photos are processed using OpenAI's advanced AI technology to provide accurate plant identification and care information.
  • Care Recommendations: We use identification results to provide personalized plant care instructions and recommendations.

App Functionality

  • Core Features: We use your information to provide core app features, save your plant collection, and personalize your experience.
  • Subscription Management: Process subscription payments and manage your premium features.
  • Notifications: Send plant care reminders and app updates (with your permission).

Service Improvement

  • Analytics: Anonymous data helps us improve our identification accuracy and app performance.
  • Feature Development: Usage patterns help us develop new features and improvements.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Your saved plants and preferences are stored locally on your device using secure iOS data storage methods.

Cloud Processing

  • Temporary Processing: Photos are temporarily processed in secure cloud environments for AI analysis.
  • No Permanent Storage: Photos are not permanently stored in our systems unless you explicitly save them.
  • Secure Transmission: All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols.

Data Protection

  • Encryption: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can access your data.
  • Regular Audits: We regularly audit our security practices and systems.

Third-Party Services

OpenAI Integration

  • GPT-4 Vision API: We use OpenAI's GPT-4 Vision API for plant identification. OpenAI processes your photos according to their privacy policy.
  • Data Handling: OpenAI does not store your images for training purposes when using their API.

Apple Services

  • StoreKit: We use Apple's StoreKit for subscription management and billing.
  • iCloud: Optional iCloud sync for your plant collection (if enabled).

Analytics Services

  • Anonymous Analytics: We may use anonymous analytics services to improve app performance.
  • No Personal Data: Analytics data does not contain personally identifiable information.

Your Rights

Access and Control

  • View Your Data: You can view and manage your saved plant collection within the app.
  • Delete Data: You can delete saved plants and reset app data through device settings.
  • Opt-out: You can disable analytics and data collection in app settings.

Contact Us

  • Questions: You can contact us with privacy questions or concerns.
  • Data Requests: Request access to or deletion of your data.
  • Complaints: Report privacy concerns or violations.

Children's Privacy

LeafLens is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
